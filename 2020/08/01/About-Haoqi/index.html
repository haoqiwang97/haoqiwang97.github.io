Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Biomedical Engineerring working with Dr. Mia Markey at The University of Texas at Austin.

I received my B.S. degree from the School of Physics at Nanjing University (NJU) in 2019. During my undergraduate study, I did research with Professor Yi Cao on single molecule biophysics, including protein-protein interactions, protein unfolding, etc. In summer 2018, I joined Professor Phillip Messersmith‘s lab in UC Berkeley, exploring molecular design principles of lysine-DOPA wet adhesion.
